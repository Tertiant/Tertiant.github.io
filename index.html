  • How can I get to the venue?

    Unfortunately, the venue is not very accessible via transit. We have put together a carpooling coordination spreadsheet , if you are looking for a ride or are able to give someone a ride, please consider signing up.

  • What is parking like?

    There are 19 reserved parking spots for our wedding in the lot closest to the building, with additional parking spots a short walk away. You are encouraged, if you are able, to park in the general parking area slightly past the venue. This allows guests who may not be able to walk the full distance to park as close as possible. Please stay safe and default to a ride service or carpooling if you plan to drink.

  • Is it okay to take pictures?

    Yes! We are more than happy for you to take photos and share them with us. However, please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony to be fully present.
